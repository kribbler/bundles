Sonic Producer is a cutting edge technology enhanced, fully functional music beat sequencer containing MP3 export capacities. The product is a comprehensive package of music production tutorials which can be accessed only by registered members of the site. If you like to create your own beats but need a high volume of options to explore your potential, Sonic Producer is a safe choice. It provides step by step guidance on the creation of beats in any type of computer or laptop.

The product is available for purchase online for a reasonable price compared to its value loaded features. It also has an appealing concept of a 60 day money back guarantee which in itself is an exceptional feature that is not seen in many other online beat making software. Sonic Producer is structured with a wide ranging set of music beat creation tutorials including a series of videos. This facilitates the production of easy and simple beats that can match up to any professional quality.

Outstanding features of Sonic Producer can be highlighted as follows:

  • Easy to use interfaces with volume controls, BPM and effects
  • Drum Pad, 16 track sequencer, synth sounds, piano key set etc.
  • Drag and drop notes from drum pad, keyboard and sequencer
  • Adjustable beats tempo
  • Easy to navigate toolbar facilitating options of saving, loading and recording projects
  • Adjustable volume levels for each channel
  • Saves beat creations to top and crisp quality MP3 format

A broad range of more than 2000 sounds from Spanish guitars, steel drums, piano, kicks, timpani, snares, timbals and many others can be availed from Sonic Producer which makes beat making a fun and exhilarating process as well as providing an end product with high quality and style.
