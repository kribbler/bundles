In 1989, Dave Hewitt discovered a need for a quality, unique snack food. Dave’s Homemade Beef Jerky is a delicious snack that will indeed fill your hunger gap. The unique texture, mild spice and desirable flavor make this a great snack for everyone to enjoy. Perfect for the people on the go and those who love the outdoors, Dave's Homemade Beef Jerky can be taken anywhere because it is shelf stable which means no refrigeration is required at any time.

Dave’s Homemade Beef Jerky is made from a carefully selected premium cut of beef. Once cut to size it is then combined and blended with the Hewitt family secret recipe of spices and then smoked to create a delicious mouth watering treat. The secret recipe results in the unique texture and flavour. This makes Dave’s Homemade Beef Jerky a very desirable, traditional snack and can be found all over Ontario.
