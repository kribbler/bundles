Resources

Anchor Recordings
Anchor Recordings distributes Bible teaching, devotional and evangelistic messages, worship cassettes and videos, principally in the UK, but also in many countries overseas.

Christian Fellowship Publishers
Publishes works by Watchman Nee and Stephen Kaung.

Christian Friends of Israel
An international evangelical Christian ministry legally registered in Israel with headquarters in Jerusalem where they operate 12 outreaches and administrate the ministry.

Christian Testimony Ministry
These messages come from the Christian Testimony Ministry in Richmond, Virginia. Most of these messages were recorded at meetings in Virginia. Other assemblies internationally have contributed to the resources on this site.

Derek Prince Ministries
The ministry of Derek Prince and resources are available on this website.

Hatikvah FilmTrust
A Christian ministry which aims to promote the advancement and understanding of the Christian Faith and of Biblical Prophecy through the production of documentary films, television and audio-visual programmes.

Intercessors for America
Founded in 1973, Intercessors for America serves the Church of Jesus Christ by encouraging effective prayer and fasting for the Church, the Nation and its leaders.

Intercessors For Israel
Intercessors for Israel seeks to raise up a prayer army in and for Israel. Their primary calling is to intercede before God in behalf of the people and Land of Israel, and to do their part in the battle to prepare the way for the return of the Lord.

International Christian Embassy Jerusalem
Founded in 1980 as an evangelical Christian response to the need to comfort Zion according to the command of scripture found in Isaiah 40:1-2: “Comfort, comfort my people, says your God. Speak tenderly to Jerusalem...”

Prayer For Israel
A fellowship of those whose first concern is to pray for Messianic and Arab believers in the land of Israel, including adjoining territories. They also provide practical support, and relieve hardship and distress in Israel and elsewhere.

Sovereign World Publishers
Sovereign World was founded by Chris Mungeam in 1986. Their vision is to publish Christian literature firmly founded on the Word of God and that rightly reflect the Inspiration of the Holy Spirit. Their world wide distribution provides the Body of Christ with quality Christian literature while serving Christian Authors with outstanding publishing service.
