Hidden Canyon in Brecksville, Ohio boasts heavily wooded lots with stunning lake and ravine views.

 

Situated near the 70 mile-long Cuyahoga River adjacent to the National Park, Hidden Canyon’s spacious one to nine acre lots will suitably accommodate your custom Kraftech home. 

 

Located just 15 minutes from downtown, Brecksville is one of Cleveland’s most exclusive and prestigious suburbs.  You’ll enjoy one of the area’s premier school systems as well as the opportunity to utilize a state-of-the art recreation and community center.
