Lake Forest Estates of Macedonia beckons with elegance, breathtaking natural beauty, rolling terrain and encompassed by nature preserve.

 

Upon entering the estates you are greeted by the illuminated adobe entranceways that guide you to the dual lakes surrounded by common grounds where residents can enjoy the park-like setting. The 170 single family estates are established with a minimum of ½ acre home sites and designed within the architectural restrictions.

Lake Forest Estates is perfectly suited for active adults and growing or established families that can experience the top "Excellent with Distinction" Rated Nordonia Hills Schools.
