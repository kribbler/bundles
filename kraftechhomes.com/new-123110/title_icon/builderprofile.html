Kraftech, Inc. is a dynamic and steadily growing custom home building company which provides innovative and enthusiastic designs at an affordable value. Paul Karnow, owner of Kraftech, utilizes his experience in the building industry to form a bond between the builder and the homeowner and continually adds new ideas to enhance his designs. We aim to provide our clients with integrity, trust and flexibility in all facets of the construction process.

 

We treat each home as an individual project with specific needs. Paul works with each homeowner, giving them personal guidance throughout their decision making and closely monitors the quality of the workmanship that goes into building the home. At completion, we want the homeowner to feel that building a home has been a gratifying experience.

 

Kraftech has designed and built a vast range of custom homes ranging from over 1000 to over 4000 square feet of finished area. We can design a home to suit your lifestyle and budget.

 

Feel free to explore our website further and we hope to answer any questions you may have. Please view Our Communities to see the latest homes available by Kraftech.
